Nigeria Hockey Federation

The Nigeria Hockey Federation (NHF) governs and controls the hockey sport in Nigeria
They are also associated to the African Hockey Federation and International Hockey Federation (IHF).

The Nigerian hockey headquarter is situated in Abuja FCT Nigeria with Sen. Abdul Ningi
as the current Nigerian Hockey President with Chimezie Asiegbu as the secretary.

Nigeria Hockey Federation (NHF) is responsible for all competitions (The Super League),
continental and intercontinental tournaments and championships and are not limited to local
tournaments.

COMPETITIONS

SUPER LEAGUE(MEN)

The Hockey Men Super League is hosted annually with six teams competing for the grand prize in a league format. At the end of all the normal league matches, the top two placed teams will play a play-off match that guarantees the ultimate winner. The top two teams also qualifies for the African Club Championship (ACC) hosted annually also. The 3rd and 4th team also play in the play-offs, while the 5th and 6th team play the final match of the play-offs, the loser of this play-off is relegated to The league one. The top performing players in this tournamnet are called up to the National team.

SUPER LEAGUE(WOMEN)

The Hockey Women Super League is hosted annually with six teams competing for the grand prize in a league format. At the end of all the normal league matches, the top two placed teams will play a play-off match that guarantees the ultimate winner. The top two teams also qualifies for the African Club Championship (ACC) hosted annually also.The top performing players in this tournamnet are called up to the National team.

THE LEAGUE ONE

The Hockey Men Super League is hosted annually with six teams competing for promotion to the Super League in a league format. At the end of all the normal league matches, the top two placed teams will play a play-off match that guarantees the ultimate winner that gets promoted to The Super LEague.

THE U-21 CHAMPIONHIP

The U-21 Championship is hosted annually with six teams competing for the grand prize in a league format. At the end of all the normal league matches, the top two placed teams will play a play-off match that also guarantees the ultimate winner. The winner of this tournament is rewarded with a cash prize and also top performing players are called up to the National U-21 team.
